Method Overview

3DGS Asset Refinement

2

SAM3-Based Door Extraction

Estimate door corner regions for module alignment.

3

Door-Based Alignment

Register room modules using four corresponding door corners.

4

Door Interaction

Keep door-region assets separate for open/close interaction.
