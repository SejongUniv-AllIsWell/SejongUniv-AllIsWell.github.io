Modutwin

Modular Indoor Digital Twins via Door-Based Alignment of 3D Gaussian Splatting Assets

Kyu-min Lee, Jae-hwan Kim, Jae-hoon Park, Seoung-jun Cho, Oh-young Song
Sejong University
Paper Demo Video Website

Room-level 3DGS assets are refined and aligned through corresponding door regions.

Refine

Remove outer and boundary-crossing Gaussians, then bake their appearance into boundary mesh textures.

Align

Use the four corners of corresponding door regions to connect room modules to the existing digital twin.

Interact

Separate door mesh and Gaussian assets as an independent element for open and close interaction.
