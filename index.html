This table evaluates appearance preservation by comparing images rendered from the original 3DGS assets and the refined assets after exterior Gaussian removal and boundary mesh setup. Images are rendered from random indoor camera viewpoints.

PSNR measures pixel-level similarity; higher is better (>30 dB: high, 25-30 dB: moderate, <25 dB: noticeable difference). SSIM measures structural similarity; higher is better (>0.90: high, 0.80-0.90: moderate, <0.80: noticeable difference). LPIPS measures perceptual difference using deep features; lower is better (<0.10: high similarity, 0.10-0.20: moderate difference, >0.20: noticeable difference).
